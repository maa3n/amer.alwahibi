Amer Alwahibi

I was born 1993 in Damascus, Syria, but my originally from Tiberias, Palestine. I was a student at the faculty of fine arts -Damascus Univeristy, Department of Printmaking until I finished the 3rd year. I made works of art in different printing techniques (intaglio- cameo - relief - screening ) and also I paint and sculpt. Now I’m studying at Minerva Academy (Fine Arts Academy) in Groningen at the third year. I shared some of my works in group exhibitions in Syria and the Netherlands :
•Spring exhibition 2013 . Damascus.
•Young exhibition 2013.Damascus.
•Belonging exhibition 2014. Damascus.
•Maas gallery 2016, Nijmegen.
•Artzanstad 2016, Zaandam.
•Podium Mozaiek 2016, Amsterdam.
•Sociëteit De Harmonie 2017, Groningen.
•Kunstwinkel 2019,Groningen.
